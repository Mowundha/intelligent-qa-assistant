Q: How do I apply for a credit card?
A: You can apply for a credit card online through our website, mobile app, or by visiting your nearest branch.

Q: How do I block my debit card?
A: You can block your debit card immediately through mobile banking app, internet banking, or by calling customer care.

Q: What is the credit card bill payment process?
A: You can pay your credit card bill through auto debit, internet banking, mobile app, or at any branch.

Q: How do I increase my credit card limit?
A: You can request a credit limit increase through internet banking or by calling customer care with your income details.

Q: What should I do if my card is lost or stolen?
A: Immediately block your card through mobile app or call customer care 24x7 helpline to report lost or stolen card.
