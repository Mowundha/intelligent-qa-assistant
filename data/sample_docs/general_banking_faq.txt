Q: What are the banking hours?
A: Banking hours are Monday to Friday 10am to 4pm and Saturday 10am to 2pm. ATMs are available 24x7.

Q: How do I register for internet banking?
A: Visit your nearest branch with ID proof to get internet banking credentials or register through our mobile app.

Q: What is KYC and why is it needed?
A: KYC stands for Know Your Customer. It is a mandatory process to verify customer identity as per RBI regulations.

Q: How do I reset my internet banking password?
A: You can reset your password through forgot password option on internet banking portal using your registered mobile number.

Q: How do I contact customer care?
A: You can reach customer care through 24x7 helpline number, email support, or live chat on our website or mobile app.
