Q: How do I apply for a personal loan?
A: You can apply for a personal loan online, through mobile app, or by visiting your nearest branch with income proof and ID.

Q: What is the interest rate for home loan?
A: Home loan interest rates start from 8.5 percent per annum depending on loan amount, tenure and credit score.

Q: How do I check my loan status?
A: You can check your loan application status through internet banking, mobile app, or by calling customer care.

Q: What documents are needed for car loan?
A: For car loan you need ID proof, address proof, income proof, bank statements of last 6 months and car quotation.

Q: How do I foreclose my loan?
A: To foreclose your loan, visit your nearest branch with a foreclosure request letter and pay the outstanding amount plus charges.
